"Hello! Sharing world"

GAME DEVELOPERS CONFERENCE SHOWCASE 2022

A joyful celebration believing in the value of openness and sharing, creating a positive change with people from over 80 countries is taking place in March, in USA.

2022-03-21(MON) ~ 25(FRI)

@ Moscone Center San Francisco, CA USA

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to the speakers from various countries about the messages of sharing and opening.

GDC Exhibition

Appreciate various creations applying GDC license of artists, organized from GDC home office.

Forum

Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with experts for each topic.

Workshop

Try creating your own work using open source license rather than just watching at it.

GDC Ignite

Get opportunities to network with GDC affiliates around the world, also after the conference.
